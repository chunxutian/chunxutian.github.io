About Me(关于我)

Chunxu Tian is a Associate Professor in the Academy for Engineering & Technology at Fudan University, working with Robots in the Advanced Robotics and Automation Laboratory.

Since June 2022, Tian have been working as an Associate Researcher at the Institute of AI and Robotics, Fudan University. From April 2019 to May 2022, Tian served as a Postdoctoral Fellow in Robotics and Mechatronics at Fudan University in Shanghai, China, under the guidance of Professor Dan Zhang (张·丹) (The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, China). Between November 2016 and November 2018, Tian pursued a Joint Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, focusing on Computer Graphics and CAD, at Stony Brook University in New York, USA, where his Ph.D. supervisor is Professor Jeffrey Ge (葛巧德) (Stony Brook University, New York, USA). Prior to that, from September 2014 to March 2019, Tian completed my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in Parallel Robotics, at Beijing Jiaotong University in Beijing, China, under the supervision of Professor Yuefa Fang (方跃法) (Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China). Tian also obtained a Master of Applied Science (MASc) in Mechanical Engineering, with a focus on Parallel Robotics, from Beijing Jiaotong University from September 2013 to July 2014, again under the guidance of Professor Yuefa Fang.


My research spans (but is not limited to) five key areas:

  • Robotic Mechanism
  • Humanoid Robots/Quadruped Robots
  • Dexterous Hands
  • Bionic UAV
  • Origami Robots
